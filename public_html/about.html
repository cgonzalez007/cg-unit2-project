 A Few Things About Us


We believe in freshly made food at a reasonable cost. We also believe in making the most authentic Cantonese, Hunan, and Szechwan-styled cuisine in the Hartland area. We only use 100% low fat vegetable oil in the food we make, and DO NOT add MSG. Come in and meet our friendly staff and see for yourself why we are rated as one of top Chinese restaurants in the state of Wisconsin.
